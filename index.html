Invisible

When you start doing things on your own, you don't need anyone, because you learn to give compassion and love to yourself. An invisible childhood is the perfect chance for that. Over time you become independent and learn not to feel, it becomes very difficult to choose to feel because there is no way to understand something you have never felt before, you can only imitate or learn.

Judger

Everyone has been uncomfortable with who I am, even though it's none of their business... but I've always been able to say that's who I am. People get involved in other people's lives because they can't bear to see their own shortcomings and other people's pluses. Suppressed lives are pathetic, social norms are garbage for the intelligent. People discover their own truth when they make their own way. Just don't care and move on, don't care about the lives of others, ask yourself are you one of those who lead their own lives or are you one of those whose lives lead them?

Industrial Designs

Professional Designs

Toyoto Boshoku Graduation Project: I-Loop

Designed I-Loop, concept for a ring car run by electric to meet the needs of the Toyoto Boshoku inter-factory travel for staff. Met the needs of passengers with relaxing music, smart windows for natural light enhancements, auto-ramps for ease of access, air-conditioning for clean air, automatic doors, and easy touch screen commands. Maximized travel time by having the car loop through a pre-determined route when necessary for passengers; the set-route also makes sure the car is driverless.
